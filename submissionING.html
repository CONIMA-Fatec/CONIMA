Submissão de Artigo

The First Conference on Intelligent Systems and Machine Learning Applications (CONIMA) is now accepting submissions for extended abstracts. Papers can be submitted in either English or Portuguese, with a limit of two pages.


The authors must register on the CMT (Conference Management Toolkit) to submit their work.

All papers will undergo a double-blind review process, so the submission must not include any author identification. Accepted papers must submit the final version with author identification and affiliations.

After approval, it will be determined whether the paper should be presented orally or as a poster.

Important dates
Event Deadline
Paper Submission October 25
Notification of approval November 4
Camera ready November 11
Oral and Poster presentation November 18

Paper submission Deadline
16/09 - 25/10

The following themes are expected, but not limited to:

  • Machine learning applied to medicine

  • Machine learning applied to food technology

  • Intelligent Management Information Systems

  • Applied natural language processing

  • Large language models and applications

  • Applied computer vision

  • Methods for building intelligent systems
